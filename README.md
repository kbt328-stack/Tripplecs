# Triple C's — Strategic Growth

A four-page branded microsite built from the Triple C's Strategic Growth Proposal, the Cape Cod 1.5 Summer Proposal, and the v2 infographic timeline.

## Pages

- **index.html** — Roadmap & Five Phases overview (the main landing page)
- **cape-cod.html** — Phase 1.5: Cape Cod Summer Pop-Up
- **valuation.html** — The Valuation Arc (from $1.4M today to the eight-figure exit)
- **the-window.html** — Why Now: the market window and competitive moat

## Brand System

All four pages share `assets/brand.css`, a single source of truth pulled directly from the v2 infographic:

**Palette**
- `--char` #1A0F08 — charred wood
- `--mahog` #722F1B — mahogany
- `--flame` #D4521A — flame
- `--ember` #E8821B — ember
- `--gold` #E8B547 — brass / gold
- `--parchment` #F2E4C1 — parchment
- `--cream` #FAF1DC — cream paper

**Typography**
- **Rye** — western display headings
- **Alfa Slab One** — slab headlines
- **Bungee Shade** — stamp accents
- **Special Elite** — typewriter eyebrows & labels
- **Crimson Pro** — body copy

Every page reuses the same sticky nav, broadside-style hero, section rules, and stamp-style footer so the four documents read as one cohesive deck.

## Local preview

Just open `index.html` in a browser — no build step. Fonts load from Google Fonts.
